# Literature Review: Quantum Random Walks in Market Microstructure

## 1. Introduction
The application of quantum probability and quantum random walks (QRW) to financial markets has gained significant traction. This review synthesizes literature across three domains: (1) Market Microstructure and Limit Order Books, (2) Quantum Finance and QRWs, and (3) Heavy-Tailed Distributions in Finance.
